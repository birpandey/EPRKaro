Who We Are at Clairgies Tech

Transforming Waste into Opportunity with Innovative Solutions

With over 2 years of experience in waste management services, We have developed innovative solutions to reduce waste and increase efficiency. Clairgies Tech is a pioneering waste management company dedicated to transforming India’s waste management sector through innovative solutions, products and sustainable practices. With a steadfast focus on Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) for plastics and advanced recycling technologies,we are committed to addressing the pressing challenges posed by plastic waste pollution. We specialize in plastic waste processing technology, converting discarded plastic into high-quality recycled materials.

  • Committed to reducing waste through efficient and innovative practices.
